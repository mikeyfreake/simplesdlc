The following SDLC processes are contained in Simple SDLC:

  • Product Backlog - track stories for all your products and have the Product Owner maintain the priorities
  • Release Backlog - group development iterations into releases
  • Sprints - attach stories to your sprints and track progress
  • Stories - capture short, simple descriptions of functionality your users request
